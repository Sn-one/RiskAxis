# REFERENCES.md

RiskAxis is designed as an objective-centered ERM platform aligned with recognized ERM and secure application development principles.

## ERM and Risk Management References

1. COSO Enterprise Risk Management — Integrating with Strategy and Performance.
   - The COSO ERM framework emphasizes the importance of considering risk in strategy-setting and performance.

2. ISO 31000:2018 Risk Management — Guidelines.
   - ISO 31000 provides principles and guidelines for identifying, analyzing, evaluating, treating, monitoring, and communicating risks across an organization.

3. Django REST Framework official documentation.
